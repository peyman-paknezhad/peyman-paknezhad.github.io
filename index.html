About Me

Hello! I'm Peyman Paknezhad, a Master's student in the Erasmus Mundus program for Image Processing and Computer Vision (IPCVai). My passion lies in building the next generation of intelligent and reliable systems, a journey that began with my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Amirkabir University of Technology.

My work centers on the intersection of deep learning and security. I am particularly drawn to the complex challenges of adversarial machine learning, AI security, and the development of trustworthy AI. The questions that drive me are: How can we make AI models robust against manipulation? And how do we ensure they operate safely and reliably? Exploring these areas, alongside my interest in bio-inspired and efficient computing, is the core of my academic ambition. I am always eager to learn, collaborate on meaningful projects, and connect with others who share my passion.
